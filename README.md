## ğŸ”´ Problem Identification
Through the initial research phase, I identified three primary drivers of dissatisfaction:
1. **Psychological Anxiety:** Patients felt "uncertain waits" were longer than "known waits" due to unclear display logic.
2. **Operational Gaps:** Lack of volunteer guidance during peak hours led to line-cutting disputes.
3. **Physical Constraints:** Limited seating and vertical window lines caused crowding and social distancing concerns.

## ğŸ›  Strategic Solutions
I developed a phased roadmap for optimization:
* **Short-term:** Redesigned bilingual signage with vivid icons and clear instructions ("Do not queue before your number is called").
* **Mid-term:** Increased volunteer deployment during peak hours to manage flow.
* **Long-term:** Designed a multi-screen integrated calling system to sync pharmacy numbers with hallway TVs, allowing patients to wait comfortably in seating areas.
